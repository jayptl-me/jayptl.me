They told me to stop playing games. So I started building them.

My journey as a developer started when I was told to quit video games—so I decided to build them instead. That passion evolved into architecting high-performance mobile apps with Flutter, where I obsess over creating buttery-smooth user experiences that feel intuitive and alive.

Look, my origin story isn't some lab accident. I used to crush levels in BGMI and Valorant until low grades hit like a boss fight I couldn't beat. Dad said "no more games," so I flipped the script: started learning 3D modeling, rendering, physics scripting – all to build my own damn games. But then it clicked – why stop at pixels when I can code apps, websites, and rake in the crypto? Boom. Coding became my superpower.

My first strike? Huselk – a full-blown e-commerce clone. Payment gateways? Integrated. Web3 coins? Minted. Full-stack glory from scratch. No disasters here; I learn fast, iterate faster, and make UIs so smooth they glide like Spidey through the city.

By day, I'm wrapping up my B.Tech at ADIT in Anand, Gujarat, but my real lab is the code editor. Primary weapon: Flutter & Dart, backed by Firebase firepower, Razorpay for seamless transactions, and databases like MongoDB, NoSQL, or MySQL holding the fort. Cloud? GCP and AWS (EC2, S3, Lambda) are my playgrounds. Design? I wireframe in Figma, polish in Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator – because ugly apps are for amateurs.

I've interned at spots like NextEdgeLabs, CodeXcelerate, and AGEVOLE, leading teams as a Flutter dev. Snagged the win at Odoo x Charusat Hackathon '25. Freelanced for Dpizza Time, built their mobile app. And at Huselk, I was the associate dev who led the e-com charge.

But here's the truth: I'm not just a coder. I'm the guy who turns "what if" into "watch this." When I'm not debugging, I'm chilling with friends and family – my real recharge. Or geeking out over Marvel movies, esports, or blasting music that swings from peaceful vibes to "devil searching for redemption" anthems. Talkative? Nah, I save my words for code that speaks volumes. Peace and silence are my secret weapons.

Dream big? Forget one app – I'm building an empire called OPSC, a suite of tools that solve problems you didn't know you had.

Ready to team up? Check my GitHub blueprints or hit up jayptl.me. Let's build something unbreakable.
