Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to my portfolio website. Your privacy is important to me. This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and protect information when you visit my website at jayptl.me (the "Website").

I am committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about the data I collect. This website is designed with privacy in mind and collects minimal data solely for understanding user engagement and improving the user experience.

Information I Collect

Analytics Data (Google Analytics)

I use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with my website. This helps me improve the content and user experience. Google Analytics collects:

  • Pages you visit and time spent on each page
  • How you arrived at my website (referral source)
  • Your approximate geographic location (country/city level)
  • Device and browser information
  • Screen resolution and device type
  • User interactions (clicks, scrolls, form submissions)

What I Don't Collect

I want to be clear about what I don't collect:

  • No Cookies: I do not use cookies for tracking or storing personal information
  • No Personal Information: I do not collect names, email addresses, or other personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily provide it through contact forms
  • No Cross-Site Tracking: I do not track your activity across other websites
  • No Behavioral Profiling: I do not create detailed user profiles for advertising or marketing purposes

How I Use Information

The information collected through Google Analytics is used solely to:

  • Understand which content is most valuable to visitors
  • Identify technical issues and improve website performance
  • Analyze user engagement patterns to enhance user experience
  • Monitor website traffic and usage statistics

I do not: sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes, use your data for advertising, or create detailed personal profiles.

Google Analytics & Data Processing

Google Analytics is configured with privacy-focused settings:

  • IP Anonymization: Your IP address is anonymized before processing
  • Data Retention: Analytics data is automatically deleted after 26 months
  • No Advertising Features: Google Ads and remarketing features are disabled
  • No User-ID Tracking: I do not use User-ID features to track individuals across sessions

Google Analytics processes data according to Google's Privacy Policy. You can learn more about how Google uses data at Google's Privacy & Terms.

Your Rights & Choices

Opt-Out Options

You have several options to control data collection:

  • Browser Settings: You can disable JavaScript to prevent analytics tracking
  • Google Analytics Opt-out: Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Do Not Track: I respect Do Not Track signals sent by your browser
  • Ad Blockers: Most ad blockers automatically block Google Analytics

Data Requests

Since I don't collect personally identifiable information, there's typically no personal data to access, modify, or delete. However, if you have concerns about your privacy or data, feel free to contact me.

Contact Forms

If you choose to contact me through any contact forms on this website:

  • I only collect information you voluntarily provide (name, email, message)
  • This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry
  • I do not store this information longer than necessary
  • I do not share this information with third parties
  • You can request deletion of this information at any time

Data Security

I take data security seriously and implement appropriate measures to protect any information processed through this website:

  • The website uses HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • No sensitive personal data is stored on my servers
  • Analytics data is processed by Google under their security standards
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

Third-Party Services

This website uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics and user engagement insights
  • Google Fonts: For typography (fonts are loaded from Google's CDN)
  • Cloudflare: For content delivery and DDoS protection

Each of these services has their own privacy policies governing how they handle data.

International Users

This website is hosted and operated from the United States. If you are accessing this website from outside the United States, please be aware that information collected may be transferred to, processed, and stored in the United States.

For users in the European Union, this processing is based on legitimate interest in operating and improving the website. You have rights under GDPR including the right to object to processing.

Children's Privacy

This website is not directed to children under 13 years of age. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact me immediately.

Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last modified" date. Significant changes will be highlighted prominently.

I encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how I protect your privacy.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or my privacy practices, please feel free to contact me:

  • Website: jayptl.me
  • Contact Form: Available on the main website

I will respond to privacy-related inquiries within a reasonable timeframe.

Summary

TL;DR: I use Google Analytics to understand how people use my website, but I don't collect personal information, use cookies for tracking, or share your data for marketing. Your privacy matters to me, and I've configured everything with privacy-first settings.
